Welcome to the Siena College Programming Competition Homepage!

Siena College's programming contest brings together talented high school students located within the Albany area to Siena's campus for a competition to test a team's programming skills. This competition is for current high school students only! There will be plenty of refreshments available and a nice prize for the winning team.

Siena's Programming competition will take place Friday, October 16th in Roger Bacon Hall.
